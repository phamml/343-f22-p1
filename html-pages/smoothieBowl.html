SMOOTHIE BOWL

Calories: 100 cal
Makes: 1 servings
Total Time: 5 minutes

Hands down one of my favorite snacks. I used to eat these smoothie bowls everyday in the fall. This smoothie bowl is packed with nutrients and uses only natural ingredients to achieve its sweetness. This recipe only takes 5 mintues to make and makes the perfect on the go breakfast or snack when you're in a hurry and need something substantial to keep you full.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Chia seeds
  • Flax seeds
  • Drink Orgain vanilla protein powder
  • Fage Greek yogurt
  • Almond milk
  • Frozen spinach
  • Frozen kale
  • Cauliflower rice
  • Banana
  • Fresh rapsberries, strawberries, blueberries, blackberries
  • Nature Pathorganic Granola

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Combine chia seeds, flax seeds, protein powder, greek yogurt, almond milk, frozen spinach, and frozen kale to a blender.
  2. Blend all ingredients together until consistency of your choice is achieved.
  3. Trasnfer smoothie to a bowl and top with fresh berries and granola. I used Naturers Pathorganic granola, but you can use any granola of your choice!
