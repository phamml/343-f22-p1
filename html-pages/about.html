HI, MY NAME IS   Natalie

Welcome to my website! My name is Natalie Nettemeyer. I am a Student-Athlete at James Madison University, majoring in Dietetics and I anticipate graduating in the Spring of 2023.

My interest in nutrition started at a young age, but it wasn't until my path began at JMU that I realized my dream career was to become a sports dietitian. Growing up playing soccer, basketball, and running cross country, I was always fascinated with the idea that good nutrition can enhance athletic performance. I am very passionate about health, sports, and wellness and would love to educate athletes on how they can improve their performance based on their dietary habits. I believe the cornerstone of living a healthy lifestyle is based on both a physical and mental balance. I aspire to educate people to become knowledgeable about food and nutrition and how to supply their bodies with the proper fuel to maximize their potential. I want people to enjoy what they eat, to live a healthy lifestyle, and most importantly to be happy about themselves.

Complete an ACEND accredited Dietetic Internship at James Madison University.

Become a Registered Dietitian. Earn my Master of Science in sports nutrition.
