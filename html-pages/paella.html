SEAFOOD PAELLA

Calories: 500 cal
Makes: 4 servings
Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

One of my favorite seafood dishes, Paella!! This is was packed with saffron infused rice, chicken, mussels, clams, shrimp, and chorizo. Featuring my aunts recipe :) It's the perfect recipe to enjoy with friends and family over the summer! This recipe can make up to 4 servings and only takes an hour and 30 minutes to make. If you enjoy cooking authentic Spanish dishes, then this is the perfect recipe for you!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Clams
  • Chicken
  • Low-sodium chicken broth
  • Saffron
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Yellow onion
  • Red pepper
  • Garlic cloves
  • Can of tomatoes
  • Spanish chorizo
  • Sweet paprika
  • Rice
  • Green peas
  • Fresh lemons
  • Parsley
  • Pepper

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Prep the vegetables: Mince the red pepper. Mince the onion. Mince the garlic.
  2. Cook the chicken, shrimp, & spanish chorizo. In your largest skillet, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken, cook until done then remove. Add the shrimp and saut√© about 1 to 2 minutes per side. Remove the shrimp and set it aside
  3. Cook the paella: In the same pan, heat olive oil on medium heat. Saute the onion and garlic until just translucent. Add the chopped red pepper. Add tomatoes and cook until the tomatoes have broken down. Stir in the stock, saffron and kosher salt. Sprinkle the rice evenly across the broth. Bring to a medium simmer and cook without stirring until liquid is absorbed.
  4. Add bag of frozen peas. Also add back in the chicken, shrimp, and spanish choruses. Add additional chicken broth, then cover pan over medium heat.
  5. In a separate pot, cook calms and oysters until done.
  6. Add the clams and oysters and serve: When the paella is done, add the clams and oysters to top of paella and squeeze the lemon wedges onto the top of the pan. Sprinkle parsley & serve with additional lemon wedges.
