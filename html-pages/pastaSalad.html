PASTA SALAD

Calories: 200 cal
Makes: 2 servings
Total Time: 30 minutes

I’ve been OBSESSED with these pasta meal preps. It is super easy to make a big batch on Monday and have lunch for the rest of the week. This is one of my favorites— Arugula, artichoke, and sun-dried tomato, red lentil pasta salad. Ingredients are posted below. This salad literally lasts me until Friday and it’s SO DELICIOUS!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Barilla red lentil pasta
  • Dole baby arugula
  • Reese quartered artichoke hearts
  • Medley tomatoes
  • Medium red onion
  • Trader Joe's Green Goddess salad dressing
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Salt/Pepper to taste
  • Garlic cloves

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Heat up a pot of water for the pasta. Once the water is brought to a boil add the lentil pasta and cook until al dente (about 7 minutes)
  2. Let your pasta cook down.
  3. While the pasta is cooling down, prep the vegetables: chop the tomatoes, onions, artichoke hearts, garlic cloves, and basil
  4. Add cooked pasta, arugula, chopped vegetables, and sun-dried tomatotes to a large bowl
  5. Add in as much Green Goddess dressing as you'd like and season with salt and pepper.
